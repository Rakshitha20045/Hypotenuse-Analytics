### STEP 2: Access NotebookLM

**Visit:** https://notebooklm.google.com

**Requirements:**
- Google account (free)
- Internet connection
- 2 minutes of your time

**Sign Up Process:**
1. Go to notebooklm.google.com
2. Click "Create New Notebook"
3. Sign in with Google
4. Name your notebook (your podcast topic)
5. Create notebook

### STEP 3: Input Your Content

**Add Content (choose one or multiple):**

**Option A: Paste Text**
- Copy-paste articles, blog posts, scripts
- Paste research summaries
- Add multiple sources
- AI will synthesize all

**Option B: Upload Documents**
- PDFs of research
- Word documents
- Google Docs links
- YouTube transcript links

**Option C: Web Links**
- Paste article URLs
- Paste blog links
- Paste research pages
- AI crawls and analyzes

**Best Practice:**
Add 3-5 sources for comprehensive podcast → better content → more detailed episode

### STEP 4: Generate Audio Overview (2 minutes)

**The Magic Happens Here:**

1. Right side dashboard → "Audio Overview" section
2. Click **"Generate"** button
3. AI processes your content
4. Creates podcast script
5. Generates natural-sounding audio
6. Produces 20-40 minute podcast episode
7. **Done!**

**Processing Time:** 2-5 minutes

**What You Get:**
- Full podcast episode (MP3)
- Two hosts bantering naturally
- Professional audio quality
- Clear, engaging conversation
- Natural pauses and laughter
- Realistic American accents

### STEP 5: Download Your Episode (1 minute)

**Download Process:**
1. Click "Download" button
2. Select format (MP3 recommended)
3. Save to computer
4. File ready for distribution

**File Details:**
- Format: MP3 (universal)
- Quality: High (320 kbps)
- Size: 50-150MB typically
- Duration: 20-40 minutes

### STEP 6: Upload to Distribution Platforms (5 minutes)

**Spotify:**
- Create Spotify for Podcasters account (free)
- Upload MP3 file
- Add cover art (3000x3000px)
- Write episode description
- Submit → Live in 24 hours
- Monetization: Yes (if eligible)

**Apple Podcasts:**
- Use Spotify RSS feed or
- Upload directly via Apple Podcasts Connect
- Add metadata (title, description, cover)
- Submit for review
- Monetization: Apple Podcasts Subscriptions

**YouTube:**
- Upload MP3 to YouTube
- Add static image or video background
- Write title, description, tags
- Enable monetization
- Goes live immediately
- Revenue from ads

**Other Platforms:**
- Google Podcasts (via Spotify)
- Amazon Music/Audible
- Stitcher
- iHeartRadio
- Podcast Addict
- Any RSS feed supported platform

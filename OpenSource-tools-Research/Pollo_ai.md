# Pollo.ai Comprehensive User Guide

**Pollo.ai** is an all-in-one AI creative suite specializing in **video and image generation**. It integrates dozens of top AI models (like Pollo 2.5, Seedance 2.0, Veo 3, Sora 2, Kling AI, etc.) into a single platform. 

The standout feature is **Pollo Agent** — an intelligent AI video agent that creates **production-ready, full videos** from simple prompts, links, or assets with minimal or zero manual editing.

---

## Overview & Key Highlights

- **Main Focus**: Text-to-Video, Image-to-Video, Video Cloning, Viral Trend Replication, UGC Ads, Product Videos, Avatars, and more.
- **Target Users**: Content creators, marketers, e-commerce sellers, social media managers, filmmakers, and brands.
- **Platforms**: Web + Mobile Apps (iOS/Android).
- **Strengths**: Multi-model access, agent-based automation, viral cloning, high consistency, fast workflow.
- **Website**: [https://pollo.ai/agent](https://pollo.ai/agent) (Agent page) | Main: [https://pollo.ai](https://pollo.ai)

Pollo.ai positions itself as a complete creative studio with separate sections for **Creative**, **Marketing**, and **Commerce** tools.

## Core Features

### 1. Pollo Agent (Main Highlight)
- Turns ideas, TikTok/YouTube links, product URLs (Amazon/Shopify), images, or text into complete videos.
- Modes include:
  - **Clone Viral Video**: Analyzes trending videos and recreates them with your content.
  - **UGC Video Ads**: Authentic-looking creator-style promotions.
  - **Story Videos**, **Explainer Videos**, **News Videos**, **Music Videos**, **Kids Story**, etc.
  - **Auto Mode**: Fully hands-off.

- No manual stitching — generates cohesive end-to-end videos.

## How Pollo Agent Works (Step-by-Step)

1. **Choose Mode** (Auto, Clone Viral, UGC Ads, Story, etc.)
2. **Input**:
   - Simple text prompt
   - Paste TikTok/YouTube link
   - Upload image/photo
   - Product URL (Amazon/Shopify)
3. **Customize**:
   - Duration, aspect ratio (9:16, 16:9, etc.)
   - Style, resolution, model
   - Performance instructions (actions, camera moves)
4. **Generate** — Runs in background.
5. **Refine** via chat (e.g., "make it more energetic" or "change background").
6. **Download** or export directly.

**Pro Tip**: Drop viral links for best results — Agent analyzes hook, pacing, and structure.

## Limits & Restrictions

- **Free Tier**: Low credits, slower queue, watermarks, limited resolutions, lower priority.
- **Concurrent Generations**: Limited by plan (1–6 parallel tasks).
- **Rate Limits**: Can be strict on free/low tiers during peak times.
- **Output Length**: Varies by model (short clips common; Agent handles longer compositions).
- **Commercial Use**: Allowed on paid plans (check terms).
- **Content Policy**: Standard AI restrictions (no illegal/harmful content).

## Best Practices & Tips

- Start with **short prompts** — Agent handles expansion.
- Use **reference links** for viral styles.
- For ads: Use product URLs for automated visuals.
- Experiment with different models for quality vs. speed.
- Use chat refinement instead of regenerating from scratch (saves credits).
- Export transcripts/subtitles when available.
- For consistency: Upload reference images.
- Monitor credit usage in dashboard.

**Prompting Tips**:
- Be descriptive: Include mood, camera angles, pacing.
- Example: "Create a UGC-style product review for wireless headphones, energetic tone, young creator in modern room."

## Comparison with VidRush.ai

- **Pollo.ai**: Broader (images + short/long videos), strong on short-form viral/ads, multi-model, Agent for automation.
- **VidRush.ai**: Focused on long-form YouTube-style narrated videos with B-roll.
- Choose **Pollo** for quick social/ads/viral content.
- Choose **VidRush** for documentary/educational long videos.

## HOW TO USE QWEN3 TTS - STEP-BY-STEP

### Step 1: Access the Platform
Visit: `huggingface.co/spaces/Qwen/Qwen3-TTS`

### Step 2: Enter Your Text
- Paste text in the input box
- Supports: 100+ languages

- Supports: Special characters, punctuation, numbers

### Step 3: Select Language & Voice
- Choose language (English, Spanish, Mandarin, etc.)

- Adjust speed (slow, normal, fast)
- Set pitch if available

### Step 4: Generate Audio
- Click "Generate" or "Convert"
- Processing: 5-30 seconds
- Download MP3 file
- Use immediately or share

## WHAT IS TRELLIS 2?

TRELLIS 2 is a **revolutionary AI-powered image-to-3D model converter** by Microsoft. Upload a single 2D image and instantly get a fully textured, realistic 3D model ready for games, 3D printing, VR, or any 3D application.

**No 3D modeling skills required.** No expensive software needed. Just upload and generate.

---

## HOW TO USE TRELLIS 2 - COMPLETE WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Access Platform
Visit: `microsoft.github.io/TRELLIS.2/`

### Step 2: Upload Your Image
- Click "Upload Image"
- Select JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Best results: Clear subject, good lighting
- Supported: Objects, people, animals, landscapes

### Step 3: Generate 3D Model
- Click "Generate"
- Processing time: 30 seconds - 2 minutes
- AI analyzes depth, texture, lighting
- Creates high-quality 3D geometry

### Step 4: Download & Use
- Download formats: OBJ, GLB, USDZ
- Import into: Blender, Unity, Unreal, CAD software
- Use for: Games, 3D printing, VR, web applications

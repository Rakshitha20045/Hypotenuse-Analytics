# cvoice.ai — Overview

**cvoice.ai** is a free AI-powered text-to-speech platform offering over **20,000 character voices** from anime, games, movies, TV shows, and celebrities. It positions itself as the world's largest character voice TTS library — completely free, with no usage limits, no credit card required, and no hidden fees.

## How It Works

Users browse or search the voice library, select a character, type their text, and generate audio instantly. A Google sign-in is required to generate audio (browsing is open to all). Output is high-quality MP3, ready to download in seconds.

## Key Features

- **20,263+ voices** spanning fictional characters, real celebrities, and public figures
- **Multi-language support** across 50+ languages
- **Free API access** with no rate limits for developers
- **Browseable by** occupation, country, or decade
- New voices added weekly

## Who It's For

Content creators (YouTube, TikTok), podcasters, game developers, educators, and developers building voice-enabled apps. Both personal and commercial use are permitted.

## Pricing

Entirely free. The team is exploring ways to sustain the platform long-term without introducing paywalls or usage caps.

# Travvir.com Guide 

**Travvir** is an AI-powered panorama and 360° camera app that lets anyone create high-fidelity immersive virtual tours and spatial experiences using only a smartphone. No dedicated 360 camera or professional equipment needed.

**Official Site**: [https://travvir.com/](https://travvir.com/)  
**Apps**: Available on iOS and Android.

---

## What is Travvir?

Travvir builds **spatial intelligence models** that turn ordinary smartphone photos into navigable 360° virtual tours, 3D room layouts, and immersive content. 

The platform combines:
- On-device + cloud AI stitching
- Neural rendering
- Advanced image enhancement
- Compression for low-latency viewing

**Core Promise**: Turn real-world spaces into digital replicas accessible via web links, embeds, or VR viewers — perfect for real estate, hospitality, retail, restaurants, offices, and more.

Trusted by companies like real estate agencies across 6 countries, Swiggy Dineout (restaurant showcases), WeWork South Africa, and others.

## Key Features

### 1. Smartphone 360° Capture
- Use your phone's camera to scan rooms or spaces.
- Guided capture mode with dot grid overlay.
- 12–16 positions per scene for seamless stitching.
- AI handles alignment, stitching, and enhancement automatically.

### 2. AI-Powered Processing
- High-fidelity 360° panoramas up to **16K resolution**.
- Automatic 3D room layout generation.
- Hyper-detailed textures and lighting preservation.
- Low latency loading optimized for mobile/web.

### 3. Virtual Tour Creation
- Multi-scene tours (connect multiple rooms).
- Add hotspots, navigation.
- Custom titles, descriptions.
- AI-generated spatial queries (chat with the space — e.g., "How far is the airport?").

### 4. Mira — AI Spatial Assistant
- Conversational AI inside tours.
- Answers questions about the space in real-time.
- Practical info: rent, location details, distances, amenities.

### 5. Output & Sharing
- Shareable public/private links.
- iframe embed code for websites.
- Short video clips from tours.
- MLS-friendly formats for real estate.
- No watermarks on free tier shares.

### 6. Additional Tools
- 3D floor plans.
- Spatial video generation.
- Analytics on viewer engagement (paid).
- White-label / custom branding (higher plans).

## How to Use Travvir (Step-by-Step Workflow)

### Getting Started
1. Download the app:
   - [iOS App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/travvir-panorama-360-camera)
   - [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.travvir.studio)
2. Sign up with email (free, no credit card needed).
3. Grant camera permissions.

### Creating Your First Tour
1. Tap **"Create New Tour"**.
2. Select space type (residential, commercial, etc.).
3. Name the tour.
4. Start capture in a room:
   - Follow on-screen dot grid.
   - Hold steady until dots turn green.
5. Repeat for each room/scene.
6. AI processes automatically (60–180 seconds per scene).
7. Preview, edit metadata, add hotspots.
8. Publish → Get instant shareable link + embed code.

**Time Estimate**: 30–60 minutes per property for a complete tour.

### Advanced Usage
- Combine multiple scans into one guided tour.
- Use Mira AI to enrich tours with contextual info.
- Embed on listing websites, social media, or client portals.
- Export short promotional videos.
- Track views and interactions (paid plans).

## Pricing & Plans (Free vs Paid)

Travvir uses a **freemium model** with in-app purchases and subscription plans. Credits available for one-time boosts.

### Free Tier
- Full AI-powered capture and tour creation.
- Hosting with shareable links.
- Embed support.
- No watermarks on shared tours.
- **Limitations**: Storage quota (limited active tours — check dashboard). Slower priority, basic analytics.

**Best For**: Individuals, occasional users, testing the platform, small businesses with low volume.

## Limits & Rate Limits

### Free Tier Limits
- Active tour storage quota (exact number shown in app — typically enough for 5–10 tours).
- Processing queue priority lower during peak times.
- Basic features only (no advanced analytics, limited branding).
- Rate limits on simultaneous captures/processing.

### Paid Tier Advantages
- Significantly higher or unlimited storage.
- Faster AI processing.
- No practical rate limits for normal use.
- Priority support.
- Higher resolution options and advanced AI features.

**General Rate Limits**:
- Capture speed depends on device (newer phones faster).
- Cloud processing has daily/hourly soft limits on free tier to prevent abuse.
- Enterprise plans remove almost all restrictions.

## Tips for Best Results

1. Capture in good, even lighting.
2. Move slowly and hold steady.
3. Scan overlapping areas between rooms for seamless connections.
4. Use tripods or stabilizers for best quality.
5. Add rich descriptions and hotspots.
6. Test embeds on your target website early.
7. Combine with high-quality photos for hybrid listings.
8. Regularly manage your tour library on free tier.

**Pro Tip**: Start with free tier on a few properties to master the workflow before upgrading.

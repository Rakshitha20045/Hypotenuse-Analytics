# VidRush.ai User Guide

**VidRush.ai** is an AI-powered platform that turns a single prompt (topic/idea) into a complete long-form YouTube-style video. It handles research, scripting, voiceover, stock footage/B-roll, motion graphics, editing, music, and final rendering.

## Access & Getting Started

- Currently in **controlled-access** phase (invite/waitlist required).
- Sign in with Google.
- No fully public free sign-up yet.
- Works best on desktop browsers.

## Core Features & Workflow

1. **Prompt Input**  
   Enter a topic (e.g., "Create a documentary about ancient mysteries"). You can also reference a video link for style.

2. **Generation Modes**
   - **Auto Mode**: Fully hands-off generation.
   - **Manual Mode**: Review/edit script before video generation.

3. **Models**: Mini and Pro (different quality/speed).

4. **Supported Video Lengths**
   - Quick: 6-8 minutes
   - Standard: 10-12 minutes
   - In-depth: 18-20 minutes
   - Documentary: 30-40 minutes

5. **Post-Generation Tools**
   - Full video editor (replace clips, adjust timeline, add CTAs, animations, custom assets)
   - Thumbnail generator
   - Voice selection (including exclusive voices)
   - Export transcript
   - Team collaboration

## Pricing & Credits (Credit-Based System)

- **~55 credits per minute** of final video.
- Covers the entire pipeline (research → script → visuals → voice → rendering).
- Re-renders cost significantly less.
- Unused credits **do not roll over**.

## Limits & Important Notes

- **Concurrent generations**: Limited by plan (1–5 at once).
- Best for **narration + B-roll** style videos (Top 10s, educational, documentaries).
- Not ideal for heavy original footage or highly creative/fictional content.
- Editing is powerful but re-renders consume credits.
- Custom voiceovers can be uploaded.

## Best Practices

- Keep prompts clear and natural (avoid command-style prefixes).
- Use reference videos for better style matching.
- Start with shorter videos to test.
- Use the editor for fine-tuning.
- Export transcripts for YouTube descriptions/SEO.
